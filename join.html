Join us

We welcome motivated scientists to work on lipid and membrane biology in innate immunity, β‑cell biology, and neuronal energy homeostasis.

Open calls

  • Postdoctoral researchers— experience in membrane biology, immunology, β‑cell physiology, or neurobiology is a plus.
  • PhD students— curiosity, rigor, and enthusiasm for quantitative biology.
  • M2/visiting students— 4–6+ month research projects.

Focus areas

  • Endosome‑anchored NLRP3 inflammasomes— assembly on PI4P‑enriched endomembranes and downstream signaling.
  • Insulin granule turnover— decision between secretion and lysosomal routing in β‑cells.
  • Hypothalamic neuron trafficking— membrane routing shaping energy‑balance circuits.

How to apply

Please email us a single PDF including:

  • Short cover letter (what you’d like to work on and why).
  • CV (with publications).
  • Contact details for 2 referees.

We review applications continuously. We are committed to a supportive, rigorous, and inclusive environment.
