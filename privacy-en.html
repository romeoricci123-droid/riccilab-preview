Data we collect

This site does not collect personal data unless you submit the contact form. The form may include your name, email address, and message, plus any information you choose to provide.

Purpose and legal basis

We use your message to respond to your request and for follow up. The legal basis is our legitimate interest in handling incoming communications with the lab.

Recipients and processors

Messages are transmitted via Formspree for delivery to the lab. Formspree acts as a processor for technical transmission and logging. The site itself is served from GitHub Pages.

Retention

Messages are retained for as long as needed to respond and for administrative or scientific follow up, then archived or deleted.

Cookies and analytics

We do not use analytics tools and we do not set tracking cookies. The site works without non essential cookies.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information in transit and storage. No system is perfectly secure. Please avoid sending sensitive information via the form.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and you may object where applicable. To exercise your rights, contact ricci@igbmc.fr and provide information that allows us to identify and process your request.

External links

We may link to third party sites. Their privacy practices are their own and we encourage you to read their policies.

Changes

This page may be updated to reflect changes to the site or to legal requirements. The update date is shown above.
